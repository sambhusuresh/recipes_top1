Fish Fry

Description

This the fav food of keralites, its easy to make and much tastier than fish curry.

Ingredients

  • fish
  • pepper
  • salt
  • oil
  • pan

Steps

  1. get the fish cuted and cleaned
  2. make paste using pepper powder and salt with some oil
  3. now paste the mixer into fish body parts
  4. take pan and put some oil and boil it
  5. Wait for 15 min get the fish mature to be eaten
  6. yeaahoo fish fry ready..
